Dakrness Falls Mod


One of the Best Mods I have played

This is a mod KhaineGB originally created to fix a few “issues” himself,
his wife and some friends felt the game had. Turns out he really enjoy modding
so it will take you longer than usual to get Iron and Steel. He also tried to
inject a little realism by locking away quite a few recipes behind perks, since
most normal folk wouldn't know how to do/ make those things.



Features

  • Multiple classes to choose from, with unique perks, and you can learn them all!
  • 6 slot backpack! 12 slot crafting queue!
  • More zombies! Cities are as dangerous as they should be, while the wilderness is quieter. Pick your home carefully!
    •



Installation Instructions (If You Don’t Use the Mod Launcher)

  • 1) Uninstall 7dtd. Open Finder, from taskbar choose Go -> Home. Open Library -> Application Support -> Steam -> steamapps -> common. Delete 7 Days to Die folder. Reinstall 7 Days to Die.
  • 2) Unzip downloaded mod. Open unzipped folder. You should see 3 folders: 7DaysToDie_Data, Data, and Mods. He will be referring to this as a “home screen”.
